# Privacy Policy for HabitPunch

**Last Updated**: January 13, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to HabitPunch. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

HabitPunch is a habit tracking application built with React Native and Expo that helps users build better habits through satisfying punch card visualizations, smart reminders, and progress tracking.

By using HabitPunch, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Data You Provide

When you use HabitPunch, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Habit Information**: Names, icons, colors, and tracking data for your habits
- **Reminder Preferences**: Notification times and reminder settings for your habits
- **Progress Data**: Punch timestamps, streak information, and completion history
- **Account Information** (if you create an account): Email address, username, authentication credentials
- **Premium Purchase Information**: Purchase receipts and subscription status (managed by RevenueCat)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, session duration, crash reports
- **Performance Data**: App performance metrics, loading times, error logs (via Expo)
- **Location Data** (optional): Approximate location for location-based habit reminders (only if you grant permission)

### 2.3 Payment Information

- Payment processing is handled securely by **RevenueCat** and your device's app store (Google Play Store / Apple App Store)
- We do not directly collect or store your payment card information
- RevenueCat provides us with subscription status and purchase validation data

### 3.4 Service Improvement

- Sending push notifications for habit reminders (if enabled)

- Notifying you of updates, new features, or important changes

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- **Primary Storage**: Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage)
- **Developer Reset Feature**: The app includes a developer menu that allows you to clear all locally stored data, resetting the app to first launch state
- **Data Persistence**: Data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually clear it

### 4.2 Optional Cloud Sync

- If you create an account, your data may be backed up to secure cloud servers
- Cloud synchronization is optional and can be disabled in settings
- Cloud data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption

### 4.3 Security Measures

- Data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- We use Expo SecureStore for sensitive data storage on devices
- Regular security audits and updates to protect against vulnerabilities

### 4.4 Data Backup

- Local device backups may include your HabitPunch data
- We recommend regularly backing up your device data
- Cloud backup (if enabled) provides additional data protection
